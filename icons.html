Documentation

Base grid

Icon content should remain inside of the live area, which is the region of an image that is unlikely to be hidden from view.

Key lines

Keyline shapes are the foundation of the grid. By using these core shapes as guidelines, you can maintain consistent visual proportions across system icons.

Key shapes

Specific keylines are present for certain shapes: circle, square, rectangle, orthogonals, and diagonals. These basic shapes help unify the icons and regulate their placement on the icon grid.

Sizes

Icons are displayed as 24 x 24 px and can extend upto 32 x 32 px. All icons are available at 24 x 24 SVGs.

Strokes

Pratima icons use a consistent stroke width of either 2dp or 1.5 dp or 1dp, including curves, angles, and both interior and exterior strokes. Based on your brand guidelines, choose and maintain a consistent stroke for all icons.

Corner radius

Corner radiuses are 2dp by default, but it can range from 0 dp to 4dp based on your brand principles. Here are examples of corner radius on a 2dp stroke.

Edge variations

Two options for edges. As per your brand principles, please choose the relevant edges to pair with the corner radius. Example Here highlights soft edges on 0-4dp corner radius, with 2dp stroke.

Colour

Pratima provides the flexibility of choosing your brand colours and style for each icon. Here are some examples of how colours can be applied on the icon. Note: you can use any colour palette that your brand supports, as long as it meets the accessibility guidelines.

Background shapes

Pratima provides the flexibility of choosing your brand colours and background shapes for each icon. FAQs

Base grid

Icon content should remain inside of the live area.

Stroke consistency

Use the same thickness across all icons to maintain consistency. Do not manipulate the thickness.

Set consistency

Use the same set of icons across all 12 icons. Eg: Do not use 2px for pay one and 1.5px for transaction history.

Themes

Pratima only supports stroked icons, do not manipulate the icons to fill or add gradients.

Success

Use tick and green circle. Any size, minimum 24 x 24 dp.

Failure

Use a white cross on a red circle.
